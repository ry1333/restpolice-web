How It Works

Four simple steps.

1

Start a workout

Open SetLock on your Apple Watch and begin your gym session.

2

Tap "Set Done"

After completing a set, tap to start your customized rest countdown.

3

Rest ends, apps auto-block

When the timer hits zero, the distracting apps you selected on your iPhone are automatically blocked.

4

Start your next set to continue

Hit "Set Done" to keep moving — blocking ends automatically when you're back to work.

FAQ

Common questions.

Does SetLock read my messages or browsing history?

No. SetLock only uses Apple's Screen Time APIs to temporarily shield apps you select. We cannot and do not read your messages, browsing history, or any content within apps.

Can I choose which apps get blocked?

Yes. You have full control over which apps are shielded. Use Apple's built-in app picker to select specific apps or entire categories. You can change your selection anytime.

What happens if my phone isn't nearby?

The Watch app works independently for timing your rest periods. App shielding commands are queued and delivered when your iPhone is reachable. Your workout data syncs automatically.

Can I disable blocking anytime?

Absolutely. You can disable app shielding entirely in Settings, use the emergency unlock button during a workout, or simply end your workout to remove all shields immediately.

Is SetLock a parental control app?

No. SetLock is designed for individuals managing their own focus during workouts. It uses the "Individual" authorization mode of Apple's Family Controls framework—you control your own device only.
