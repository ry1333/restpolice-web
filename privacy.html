Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2025

Overview

Rest Police is a workout rest timer app for Apple Watch and iPhone. It optionally uses Apple's Screen Time APIs to help you stay focused during workouts by temporarily shielding distracting apps you select.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Screen Time & Family Controls

We use Managed Settings / Screen Time APIs only to apply app shields you select during workouts; we do not access or transmit device activity or app usage history.

Specifically:

  • You choose which apps to shield using Apple's built-in app picker
  • Shields are applied temporarily when your rest timer ends
  • Shields are removed when you log your next set or end your workout
  • We use the "Individual" authorization mode—you control only your own device
  • Rest Police is not a parental control app

Data We Collect

Rest Police stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Workout sessions: Start time, end time, set timestamps, rest duration preset
  • Settings: Rest timer presets, app blocking preferences
  • App selection: Which apps you've chosen to shield (stored as opaque tokens, not app names)

If you sign in with Apple, your workout data may sync across your own devices via our cloud service. We do not share this data with third parties.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Screen Time activity logs or reports
  • App usage history or statistics
  • Screenshots or screen recordings
  • Messages, emails, or browsing history
  • Location data
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Health data (we use HealthKit only to keep the Watch timer active)

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share device or app usage data with third parties. Your workout data is yours.

Data Security

Data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's built-in security. Data synced to our cloud service is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Deleting the app from your devices
  • Requesting account deletion by emailing us

Changes to This Policy
